Buffalo Multi-Omics Sequencing Data

Hello! This is a website for sharing data from Haizi (HZ) and Xuyi (XY) mountain Buffalo multiomics analysis.

As the typical swamp buffalo, XY mountain buffaloes are bred in the areas of Jiangsu province along Yangtze valley characterized by numerous hills and fewer plains, and developed through long-term selection by local mountain farmers. HZ buffaloes are raised in the similar regions to XY buffaloes but in the coastal areas

We are delighted to have you here! Explore the comprehensive analysis results of genotype, gene expression, and molecular phenotype of HZ and XY buffalo. We are eager to connect with fellow scientists interested in our project. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us. Thank you!
